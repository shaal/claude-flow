# The Future of Live-Events Apps: 2025-2045

## A Comprehensive Research Report for Konferenco

**Research Date:** January 2026
**Scope:** Technology trends, social evolution, and key innovations in event technology over the next 20 years

---

## Executive Summary

The live-events industry is undergoing a fundamental transformation driven by advances in artificial intelligence, extended reality (XR), brain-computer interfaces, and decentralized technologies. This report synthesizes research from industry reports, academic studies, and technology roadmaps to project how event experiences will evolve through 2045.

**Key Findings:**
- The XR market is projected to grow by 1,295% between 2024 and 2032, reaching an estimated $520 billion
- 54% of tech experts predict the metaverse will be a fully immersive aspect of daily life for 500+ million people by 2040
- AI-powered event matchmaking is already delivering 44% increases in in-person meetings
- Micro-events (under 50 attendees) are emerging as a 2026 megatrend
- Holographic telepresence will be a boardroom expectation for Fortune 500 companies by 2030
- Brain-computer interfaces are being explored by major tech companies for consumer XR integration

### 2.3 Micro-Events Within Macro-Events

**Definition:** Smaller-scale events with fewer than 50 participants prioritizing personalization, exclusivity, and high-value experiences.

**Format Evolution:**
- Executive dinners and customer roundtables
- Invite-only workshops
- Small-group sessions within larger conferences

**Value Proposition:**
- "Ten highly engaged attendees learning from one another can deliver far more value than a packed room with shallow interaction"
